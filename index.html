Scenario Based Learning

Mr. Rand Case

This interactive case has been developed to help you understand and strengthen your clinical reasoning skills. In this case you are the nurse caring for Mr. Rand. You will get to know Mr. Rand's history and clinical situation, and work to deliver quality care to him.

The case is set up in chronologically ordered parts, "Admission" (Part 1), "Hospital Day 3-4" (Part 2), and "Team Consult" (Part 3). In each part, clinical information will be given and you (as the nurse) will need to analyze the situation and answer questions.

Which part would you like to start?

  • Part 1: Admission
  • Part 2: Hospital Days 3-4
  • Part 3: Team Consult
